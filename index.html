Who are we

SENECA is the Swedish Network for Early Career Astronomers and is organised by early-career astronomers, for early-career astronomers. SENECA is open to all PhD students and post-doctoral researchers in Sweden of all fields of astronomy!

The main objective of SENECA is to form a strong network between early-career astronomers in Sweden by organising relevant events and engaging in different projects. Our hope is that by creating a space specifically set up for early career scientists, we will have the opportunity to hear about each other’s research, create some collaborations, and most importantly, make some friends!

We also hope to be a safe and inclusive space, as well as a resource and support network for new arrivals that may have questions about adjusting to life in Sweden.
Välkommen till Sverige!

SENECA will officially be launched during Astronomdagarna 2022 in Gothenburg.
